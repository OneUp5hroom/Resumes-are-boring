About this site

This site was created on a whim when I wanted to update my normal, vanilla, paper resume. Since my profession is in Development and most jobs I will ever look for in the future will be in this field, I decided to dedicate an entire website to the idea. This way I can show off my development ability, while showing my normal resume items in an effecient way.
This website was built in one afternnon on 4/25/2021, and I will be updated in the future with many more fun items!
